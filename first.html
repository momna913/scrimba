All about planet

A planet is a large round object that orbits a star, like the Sun. Planets do not make their own light; instead, they reflect the light of the star they orbit. In our solar system, there are eight planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Each planet is different in size, appearance, and conditions, with some being rocky and others made mostly of gas.
